Whackamole

A delicious mole based dish

An interesting mole dish made with mole, potatoes, garlic and lots more.

Ingredients

  • Mole
  • Onions
  • tomatoes
  • garlic
  • radish
  • carots
  • mushrooms
  • green peppers
  • capsicum

Steps

  1. Find a mole hole
  2. Jump up and down until a mole appears
  3. whack the mole
  4. Add the mole to the other Ingredients in a pot
  5. Slow cook for several days
  6. serve with strong Polish lager
  7. Enjoy your Whackamole

Vegitarian option

Swap mole for more carrots
