Chicken Balti

A Chicken Balti

A spicy chicken dish made with onion, tomatoes, garlic and lots more.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • tomatoes
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • coriander
  • mushrooms
  • green chilys
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Chop up onions and fry in a pan with 5 tbl spoons of rapeseed oil
  2. When onions have browned, add choped ginger and garlic
  3. Chop and add the tomatoes
  4. Add spices and green chili
  5. Add chicken
  6. Cook it all up
  7. Eat the balti
